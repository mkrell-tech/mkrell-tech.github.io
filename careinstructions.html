Judy's Daily Schedule

  1. Wake up around 9:00am.
  2. Go for a walk within an hour of waking up.
  3. Breakfast after walk.
  4. Nap and hang out until the afternoon.
  5. Second walk any time between 3:00pm to 5:00pm.
  6. Dinner after second walk.
  7. Last walk at 10:00pm.
  8. Bedtime.

Feeding Instructions

Judy gets one scoop of food in the morning and one in the evening. The food is in a white bin in the pantry. Keep her water full all day.

Walking Instructions

Judy goes on three walks per day. The first two are around 30 minutes long and the night walk is shorter - just around the block. Her leash and harness are hanging on a hook by the front door. We wash her feet after every walk since she's allowed on all the furniture.

Other Information

  • Judy likes to sleep under a blanket. Her green one is on the couch.
  • Her treats and bones are in the basket under the kitchen table.
  • She refuses to walk up the stairs so you may have to carry her unless you can coax her with treats.
